INVOGEN

Imagine you have a mobile mini POS, which you can use to generate invoices, add inventory, maintain the history of invoices and sharing these invoices with your customer.

Invogen helps small scale businesses to add their inventory and then generate sale invoices placing their business logo on it. It allows user to pick from multiple templates which they want their invoice to generate into.
